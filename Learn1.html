Delicious Pancakes

Learn how to make fluffy and delicious pancakes !

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoon melted butter

Intrustion

  1. Mix flour,backing powder and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Whisk the egg,milk, and butter in another bowl.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredient until smooth.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubble from, them flip and cook other side.
  6. Serve with syrup or toppings of your choise.
